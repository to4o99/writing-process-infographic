From Idea to Final Product

The 4-Step Writing Framework

Phase 1: The "Brain Dump"

Mindset: Explorer

"Organize the chaos."

  • Capture: Write down the elevator pitch immediately.
  • Research: Gather facts and quotes. Stack your bricks.
  • Outline: Create the map (Intro → Points → Conclusion).

Phase 2: The "Ugly Draft"

Mindset: Builder

"Volume over quality. Turn off the editor."

  • Speed: Write fast. Ignore spelling. Don't look back.
  • Placeholders: Use [TK] or [Stat Here] instead of stopping.
  • Completion: Don't edit until the last period is typed.

Phase 3: The Surgical Phase

Mindset: Architect

"Switch to critic mode."

  • Cool Down: Step away for hours or a day. Get fresh eyes.
  • Macro-Edit: Check flow, pacing, and logic first.
  • Kill Darlings: Cut beautiful sentences that don't serve the point.

Phase 4: The Polish

Mindset: Janitor

"Precision and presentation."

  • Line Edit: Fix passive voice and weak adverbs.
  • Read Aloud: If you stumble, the reader will stumble.
  • Final Proof: Read backwards to catch sneaky typos.
